The Photographic Journey

How the pipeline transforms basic product shots into professional e-commerce visuals

01

Raw Upload

Drop a raw photo of your product from any device. The canvas uploads instantly to Supabase Cloud Storage.

02

AI Copywriting

Gemini analyzes the image context, drafting design copy, categories, e-commerce tags, and precise material specifications.

03

Inpainting Renders

The system renders 4-6 custom scenes. The product details, design, and labels stay 100% identical; only the background transforms.

04

Storefront Ready

Campaign renders sync directly with Supabase DB and are served via public CDN links ready for WooCommerce or Shopify.
